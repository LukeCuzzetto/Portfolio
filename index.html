backstory.txt

From the time I was 10 years old, I was playing games on my family's computer — immersing myself in the stories and worlds built by past developers. Gaming became a safe haven.

As I grew older, I began to wonder how these games were actually made. I started learning programming and game development after high school, and I was hooked. I knew I wanted to create worlds that other people like me could sink hours into without remorse.

I graduated from Thompson Rivers University in December 2024 with a Bachelor of Computer Science, and am actively pursuing a career in Game Development.
